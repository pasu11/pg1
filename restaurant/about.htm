Having been around since 1997, Penguin Buffet has been one of the most famous chinese buffets in Great Toronto Area and has won the "Top 10 Toronto Restaurant Award" on 1997, 00, and 03.  
     
  Unlike most buffets, we offer a flat rate for all our buffet at only $12.99 per person. We have over 100 types of food and dessert all the time. Our Dim Sum is one of the best in Southern Ontario and our chiefs have won many internation cooking awards.  
 
   
  Clean environment, chinese royal style decoration and traditional bamboo music playing, you will have an unforgetable experience that we are sure you will come back another time.
