North Island

 

Beautiful beaches,mountains,wildlife, scuba diving, and festivals run throughout the summer, North Island is definately a must-visit place.

Cape Scott:

Cape Scott Provincial Park has 64 Km (40 miles) of oceanfront and 23 Km (14 miles) of beaches. It has great views along the beaches.



Telegraph Cove:

Telegraph Cove, a tiny hamlet where most of its buildings are raised above the water on pilings. Surrounded by pristine islands, mountains and wildlife, Telegraph Cove is one of the most beautiful places in Vancouver Island.



Campbell River:



Whale watching, scuba diving, and festivals run throughout the summer, Campbell River is definately a very good place to visit.
